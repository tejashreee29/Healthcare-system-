💊 Healthcare & Mental Health Tips

Your ultimate guide to a healthy lifestyle

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8-10 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration boosts digestion, skin glow, and overall health.

🥗 Balanced Diet

Include fresh vegetables, fruits, lean proteins, and whole grains in your daily diet. Avoid excess sugar and processed foods.

🏋️‍♀️ Regular Exercise

Exercise for 30 minutes daily—walking, running, yoga, or cardio to keep your body fit.

😴 Proper Sleep

Ensure you get 7-9 hours of quality sleep to boost mental and physical health.

🧠 Mental Health Care

Practice mindfulness, reduce screen time, talk to loved ones, and engage in positive activities for mental peace.

🚑 Emergency First Aid

Keep a first-aid kit at home. Learn CPR techniques for emergencies. Always have emergency contact numbers saved.

🩸 Blood Pressure Control

Check your blood pressure regularly and avoid excessive salt intake. Exercise and proper diet are key.

🦷 Dental Health

Brush twice daily, floss regularly, and visit the dentist every 6 months for oral hygiene.
