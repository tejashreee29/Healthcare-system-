Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use the AI Chatbot?

Our AI chatbot can assist you by answering questions about symptoms, providing medical history tracking, and offering healthcare tips. Simply start a chat, describe your symptoms, and receive personalized advice.

What is the Symptoms Checker?

The Symptoms Checker helps you input your symptoms and receive potential solutions or advice. Please note that the information is for educational purposes and should not replace professional medical advice.

Can I track my medical history?

Yes, you can fill out a medical history form to keep track of your health details. This will help you easily reference important information during consultations with your healthcare provider.
